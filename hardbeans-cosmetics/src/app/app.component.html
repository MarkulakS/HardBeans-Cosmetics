Coffee Grounds Oil

Hard Beans Coffee grounds oil with versatile use is an essential element of any successful SPA ritual carried out at home. It is beneficial both to the body and the hair. It has strong nourishing, regenerating and moisturizing properties, and also perfectly cares for the skin thanks to the numerous vitamins and minerals present in its formula. It creates a protective barrier on the skin, thus helps the chapped skin with a tendency to irritation. It perfectly smoothes and softens the skin, and additionally protects against water loss.

Usage:

Method 1: Shake well before use. Combine the olive with coffee grounds in a 1: 1 ratio (or other, depending on your personal preferences). Apply a small amount of the product to previously wetted skin, massage in circular movements. Rinse with warm water and gently dry the skin.
Method 2: Apply on hair - use as a hair oiling product
Method 3: Apply to the chin - use as a beard oil

Ingredients:

Persea Gratissima Oil, Prunus Amygdalus Dulcis Oil, (coffee lipids), Mauritia Flexuosa Fruit Oil, Adansonia Digitata Seed Oil, Sclerocarya Birrea Seed Oil, Passiflora Edulis Seed Oil, Parfum, Tocopherol, Helianthus Annuus Seed Oil
